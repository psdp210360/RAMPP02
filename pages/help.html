2. 🧩 RAM Structure Explained

• L0 → High-level domains (Strategy, Operations, Finance…) • L1 → Core processes • L2 → Detailed workflows • L3 → Tasks, templates, and SAP execution 👉 This structure ensures clarity, scalability, and governance

Step 2 Explore Processes

Navigate to L1 and L2 levels.

Step 3 Access Documents

Open SOPs, templates, and forms.

Step 4 Execute via SAP

Follow system steps and workflows.
