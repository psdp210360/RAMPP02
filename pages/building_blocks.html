🧩 RAM Building Blocks Framework

The RAM system is structured around core building blocks that ensure governance, efficiency, and integration across strategy, operations, and systems.

---

1. Governance & Control

Governance RACI Matrix

Defines roles and responsibilities across processes to ensure accountability.

Owner → Responsible → Accountable → Consulted → Informed
Authority Delegation of Authority (DoA)

Defines approval limits for financial, operational, and contractual decisions.

Example: CAPEX approval thresholds by management level
Compliance Policies & SOPs

Standardized procedures ensuring consistency and regulatory compliance.

---

2. Performance & Analytics

Performance KPI Framework

Defines measurable indicators for each L0 and L1 process.

Examples: Occupancy Rate, NOI, Maintenance SLA
Reporting Dashboards

Real-time monitoring of asset, financial, and operational performance.

Data Data Governance

Ensures data accuracy, ownership, and consistency across systems.

---

3. Process & Workflow

Process BPMN / Process Mapping

Visual representation of workflows from L0 to L3.

Used in: Leasing, Maintenance, Investment flows
Workflow Approval Flows

Structured approval steps embedded in operations and SAP.

Standardization Templates & Forms

Standard forms for consistency (leases, contracts, reports).

---

4. Systems & Integration

System SAP Integration

Core ERP backbone linking finance, leasing, maintenance, and projects.

Modules: FI, CO, RE-FX, PM, MM
Digital Automation

Workflow automation and digital approvals.

Data System Integration

Connecting SAP with BI tools, CRM, and external systems.

---

5. Risk & Compliance

Risk Risk Management

Identification, mitigation, and monitoring of risks.

Audit Internal Audit

Ensures processes are followed and controls are effective.

Control SAP GRC

Ensures access control, segregation of duties, and compliance.

---

🎯 How These Blocks Work Together

• Governance defines WHO does WHAT • Processes define HOW work is done • Systems (SAP) execute the work • KPIs measure performance • GRC ensures control and compliance 👉 Together → Full RAM Operating System
