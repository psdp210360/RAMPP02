RAM Policies & Procedures Manual and Knowledge Hub

Welcome to the Real Asset Management (RAM) Policies and Procedures Portal. This platform serves as a centralized knowledge hub designed to organize, standardize, and communicate all processes governing asset management, operations, and supporting functions.

Purpose of the Portal

The RAM Portal provides a structured and accessible framework covering all core and supporting processes across the organization. It ensures consistency, transparency, and operational excellence by aligning all stakeholders with clearly defined procedures and governance standards.

Portal Structure Overview

  • Level 0 (L0): Core domains representing major business functions
  • Level 1 (L1): Key processes under each domain
  • Level 2 (L2): Detailed process workflows and steps
  • Level 3 (L3): Work instructions, templates, and execution details

What You Will Find

  • End-to-end process definitions across all RAM functions
  • Standardized templates and operational forms
  • Process maps and workflows
  • Governance elements including RACI, DoA, and KPIs
  • Integration with SAP and supporting systems

How to Use This Portal

  • Use the left navigation menu to browse domains and processes
  • Start from L0 domains and drill down into detailed processes
  • Open process pages to view documentation, links, and supporting materials
  • Refer to the Process Map for a full visual overview
  • Access Resources for templates, SAP references, and tools

Why This Portal is Important

  • Ensures consistency and standardization across operations
  • Improves efficiency and reduces process ambiguity
  • Supports governance, compliance, and audit readiness
  • Enhances decision-making through structured information
  • Serves as a single source of truth for RAM processes

Note

This portal is continuously evolving. Users are encouraged to follow defined processes and refer to updated materials regularly to ensure alignment with the latest standards and practices.
