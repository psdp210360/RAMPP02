📘 About RAM Policies & Procedures

The RAM Policies & Procedures Manual (RAM-PROC-00) is a structured governance framework that defines how Real Asset Management operates across strategy, investment, operations, and corporate services.

---

🎯 Purpose of RAM

• Standardize operations across all business units • Ensure governance, compliance, and accountability • Align processes with SAP and digital systems • Enable performance tracking through KPIs • Provide a scalable and structured operating model
---

🧩 RAM Structure (Process Hierarchy)

L0 – Business Domains

High-level functional areas (Strategy, Operations, Finance, etc.)

L1 – Core Processes

Key business processes within each domain

L2 – Procedures

Step-by-step workflows and activities

L3 – Work Instructions

Detailed execution guidelines

L4 – Forms & Templates

Standardized documents supporting execution

---

⚙ How RAM Works

• Governance (RACI, DoA) defines responsibilities • Processes (L0–L3) define workflows • Systems (SAP) execute operations • KPIs track performance • GRC ensures control and compliance 👉 Together → Integrated Operating Model
---
