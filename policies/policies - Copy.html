📘 RAM Policies & Governance Framework

This page consolidates all governing policies across RAM value streams (L0-1 to L0-5), ensuring alignment with strategy, compliance, risk management, and operational excellence.

L0-1 Strategy & Portfolio Management
Corporate Strategy Policy
  • Defines long-term direction and investment priorities
  • Aligns portfolio with shareholder objectives
Portfolio Governance Policy
  • Defines asset allocation rules and segmentation logic
  • Ensures balanced risk-return portfolio
Risk Management Policy
  • Identifies strategic and financial risks
  • Defines mitigation frameworks
Data Governance & Records Policy
  • Ensures data integrity and audit readiness
  • Defines retention and documentation standards

Applies to: L1-1.1, L1-1.2, L1-1.3

L0-2 Investment & Capital Projects
Investment Policy
  • Defines investment criteria and approval thresholds
  • Ensures economic viability
CAPEX Policy
  • Controls capital expenditure planning and approvals
  • Ensures budget discipline
Procurement & Contracting Policy
  • Defines vendor selection and contracting rules
  • Ensures transparency and compliance
Project Management Policy
  • Defines project lifecycle governance
  • Ensures delivery within scope, cost, and time
Records Management Policy
  • Ensures proper documentation of all investments

Applies to: L1-2.1 → L1-2.4

L0-3 Commercial Management
Leasing Policy
  • Defines leasing rules and contract standards
Pricing & Discount Policy
  • Controls pricing approvals and incentives
KYC / Customer Due Diligence Policy
  • Ensures compliance with tenant verification
Contract Management Policy
  • Standardizes contract lifecycle management
Records Management Policy
  • Ensures audit trail of commercial activities

Applies to: L1-3.1 → L1-3.4

L0-4 Asset Operations & Tenant Management
Property Management Policy
  • Defines site operations and service standards
Facilities & Maintenance Policy
  • Defines preventive and corrective maintenance
HSE Policy
  • Ensures safety, compliance, and incident management
Service Charge Policy
  • Defines billing and cost recovery rules
Vendor Management Policy
  • Controls contractor performance and compliance
Records Management Policy
  • Ensures traceability of operations and incidents

Applies to: L1-4.1 → L1-4.5

L0-5 Corporate Services
Financial Management Policy
  • Ensures financial integrity and reporting accuracy
Procurement Policy
  • Defines sourcing and vendor governance
Human Resources Policy
  • Manages workforce lifecycle and compliance
IT & Information Security Policy
  • Ensures system security and availability
Legal & Compliance Policy
  • Ensures regulatory adherence and legal protection
Risk Management Policy
  • Monitors enterprise-wide risks
Records Management Policy
  • Defines documentation and retention standards

Applies to: L1-5.1 → L1-5.5
