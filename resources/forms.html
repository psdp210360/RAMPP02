📁 RAM Forms & Templates Library

This section provides standardized forms and templates used across all RAM processes. Forms are structured according to L0 (domains) and L1 (processes) to ensure consistency, governance, and operational efficiency.

---

🎯 Purpose of Forms

• Standardize data collection and reporting • Ensure compliance with policies and procedures • Support SAP and system integration • Enable audit trails and approvals • Improve efficiency and reduce errors
